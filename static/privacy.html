Privacy
Policy.

Your data integrity and professional privacy are our highest priorities.

1. Data Collection

When you use TailoredResume.ai, we collect the personal information you provide in your Master Resume JSON and the Job Descriptions you upload. This data is used solely for the purpose of generating your tailored resume and calculating your ATS score.

2. Use of Gemini AI

Our platform utilizes Google Gemini AI to analyze and tailor your resume content. Your data is transmitted to Google APIs for processing. We do not use your personal information to train any AI models.

3. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your Master Resume data or finished PDFs on our servers longer than necessary to provide the service. Temporary files are routinely cleared from our system.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data at any time. Since we do not maintain long-term accounts, most data is cleared automatically upon completion of your session.
